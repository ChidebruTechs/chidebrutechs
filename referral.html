Earn Rewards by Referring Chidebru Techs!

At Chidebru Techs, we believe that our clients are our biggest advocates. To show our appreciation for spreading the word about our services, we’ve launched an exciting Referral Program that allows you to earn rewards when you refer new clients to us!

How It Works

  1. Refer a Friend or Business

    2. Know someone who could benefit from our range of digital services, including branding, web design and development, digital marketing, or mobile app development? Send them our way!

  2. They Sign Up

    3. When your referral becomes a client and signs up for one of our services, you’re on your way to earning rewards.

  3. Earn Your Reward

    4. Once your referral’s project is completed or service is activated, you’ll receive your reward.

Who Can Participate?

Anyone can participate! Whether you're an existing client, partner, or simply someone who knows a business looking for expert tech solutions, you can earn rewards by joining our referral program.

What Are the Rewards?

We offer a tiered reward system based on the value of the services your referral signs up for:

  1. $50 Referral Bonus: When your referral signs up for services valued between $350 - $999.

  2. $100 Referral Bonus: When your referral signs up for services valued between $1000 - 2,499.

  3. $250 Referral Bonus: When your referral signs up for services valued between $2,500 - $4,999.

  4. $500 Referral Bonus: When your referral signs up for services valued over $5,000..

What Services Are Eligible for Referral?

You can refer clients to any of our services, including:

  • Branding and Logo Design
  • Web Design and Development
  • Content Writing
  • Digital Marketing
  • Cybersecurity Solutions
  • E-Commerce Solutions
  • Mobile App Development
  • Custom Software Development
  • IT Consulting

How to Submit a Referral

It’s easy to refer someone to Chidebru Techs! You can refer clients to any of our services, including:

  1. Complete the Referral Form: Fill out our Referral Form with your details and your referral’s information.

  2. We’ll Reach Out: Our team will reach out to your referral to schedule a consultation and assess their needs.

  3. Track Your Referral: You can track the status of your referral and rewards by contacting us directly.

Program Terms & Conditions

  • The referred client must sign a contract and complete the initial payment before the referral bonus is issued.
  • Referrals must be new clients to Chidebru Techs (not an existing customer).
  • You cannot refer your own business or company.
  • Rewards are paid out via MPESA, bank transfer, PayPal, or your chosen payment method.
